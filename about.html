Our Story

Building Trust Through Spotless Service

Founded in 2015 in Malindi, Kenya, Malindi Cleaners started with a simple mission: to provide world-class cleaning services that families and businesses can rely on. What began as a small team of three has grown into a company of 45+ dedicated professionals serving the entire Kenyan coast.

We believe that a clean environment is the foundation of a healthy, productive life. That's why we invest in continuous training, eco-friendly products, and the latest cleaning technology to deliver results that speak for themselves.
